Ablaze Labs

Ablaze Labs is our venture studio where we transform unique ideas into strong independent companies. Using our own technical and business team as well as unique operational and management techniques we build multiple companies from the ground up.

We do this by working with different Entrepreneurs that have an idea, but can’t launch and scale the startup on their own because they need help with building the product, with sales, with marketing, and with growth/ scale objectives. We will either take an idea or a vision and build it from scratch or we will collaborate with entrepreneurs and become their technical or business co-founders.

Depending on the services required ranging from market studies, projections, business planning and investment to simple consulting and partnership the lab will take equity in the company which varies from one to another based on what the startup needs.

Sand Box

A sandbox is a small space filled with sand where children play and use their imagination, build sandcastles, tunnels, etc.

For us, sandbox is a place for research and development. It’s a part of the company where we dedicate our resources towards experimentations on emerging technologies and business ideas.

Here we aim to create an environment where our employees can interact, improvise, and observe one another work on different products which might possibly make tomorrow.

The metaphor is about a place where you can play without consequences, and where you can destroy and rebuild your successive imperfect attempts at creating something.

The sandbox is a place where we test the viability of business ideas, manufacture and design different concepts of AI, Machine learning, blockchain, etc. In addition to production, we get involved in research regarding these concepts be it for us or outsourced from our clients upon request.
